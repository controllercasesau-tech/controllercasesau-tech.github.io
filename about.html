About ControllerCases

Hi, I’m the creator of ControllerCases. I’m a Perth-based UWA MD Class of 2025 graduate and incoming Intern at Sir Charles Gairdner Hospital (2026). These cases were originally designed for my own use.

I use small controllers for study tools such as Anki, and I wanted a durable, compact way to carry them without worrying about damage or accidental button presses. When I couldn’t find anything that fit properly, I started designing my own.

ControllerCases was created to solve a simple, practical problem: protecting small controllers properly during everyday use.

What began as a small personal project has grown into a side business focused on simple, functional, and well-made controller cases. All cases are designed, 3D printed, and finished locally in Western Australia using high-quality PLA+, and are printed to order rather than mass-produced.

If you have a question, a custom request, or an idea for a controller that could use a better case, feel free to get in touch.
